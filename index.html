Shazia Imam

I am a Data & Research professional at BOOM. I study the trends in Misinformation in India registered as fact-check queries from the public. I also assist academic research projects in Bihar and across. I have done a Masters in Economics from Jamia Millia Islamia and a Bachelors in Economics with Mathematics and Statistics from Aligarh Muslim University, India. I am interested in Development Economics with intersections across Gender & Minorities, Resource Economics and Energy politics. 

I am on a voyage to find myself. I am a first-gen learner from a Middle class Muslim family in Bihar(India). I am learning and growing each day so what you see here may be transient. I love to experience new places, explore more creations, meet new people and cultures. .

Academically, my aim is to pursue a Ph.D. in Economics. I love the idea of research, mostly because the profession keeps you in a university forever. I would trade all my riches to be at a place that never stops thinking.
